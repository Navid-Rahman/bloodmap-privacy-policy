Important Notice: BloodMap is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use our mobile application. By using BloodMap, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to BloodMap ("we," "our," or "us"). BloodMap is a life-saving mobile application that connects blood donors with recipients through real-time location-based matching. We understand the sensitive nature of health-related information and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you register and use BloodMap, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Full name
    • Phone number (verified)
    • Date of birth (to verify you are 18 years or older)
    • Blood group (self-declared)
    • Weight (for donation eligibility)
    • Emergency contact information
  • Profile Information:
    • Last donation date
    • Availability status
    • Preferred contact method
    • Profile photo (optional)
  • Communication Data:
    • Messages sent through our in-app chat system
    • Emergency alert details (patient name, hospital, urgency level)
    • Feedback and support communications

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Information: With your explicit consent, we collect your device's precise GPS location to:
    • Show you nearby blood donors or recipients
    • Send emergency alerts to donors in your area
    • Calculate distance for matching purposes
  • Device Information:
    • Device type and operating system
    • Unique device identifiers
    • Mobile network information
    • App version and usage statistics
  • Usage Data:
    • App features accessed
    • Time spent on different screens
    • Response times to alerts
    • Donation and request history

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Emergency Matching: To connect blood donors with recipients based on blood type compatibility and proximity
  • Alert System: To send real-time notifications about urgent blood requirements
  • Communication: To enable direct messaging between donors and recipients
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your user account
  • Verification: To verify your phone number and ensure account authenticity

3.2 Secondary Purposes

  • Safety and Trust: To maintain platform integrity and prevent fraud or misuse
  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve our services
  • Gamification: To track donation history, achievements, and progress
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Location Privacy Protection

Our Location Fuzzing System

We take your location privacy seriously. Your exact location is NEVER shown to other users. Instead, we implement the following privacy protections:

  • Radius-based Display: We show donors within 2-5 kilometer radius circles, not exact points
  • Area Clustering: Nearby donors are grouped into general area clusters
  • Random Offset: We add a random 500m-2km offset to your actual location
  • General Area Names: We display neighborhood or district names instead of specific addresses
  • No Street Addresses: Street-level location data is never displayed on the map
  • Zoom Restrictions: Map zoom is limited to prevent exact location detection

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 What We Share with Other Users

When you use BloodMap, other users can see:

  • Your first name and last initial (e.g., "John D.")
  • Your blood group
  • Approximate area location (2-5 km radius)
  • Availability status (online, offline, busy)
  • General area name (e.g., "Dhanmondi Area")
  • Response time rating (Fast, Moderate, Slow)
  • Trust score (when available)
  • Last active status (online, 1 hour ago, etc.)

5.2 What We NEVER Share

  • Your full name (until you initiate chat)
  • Your phone number (until both parties consent)
  • Your exact address or GPS coordinates
  • Building or street-level location
  • Personal medical history
  • Your exact age (we only show "Adult")

5.3 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication services
  • Google Maps/OpenStreetMap: Map services and location display
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notification delivery
  • Analytics Providers: App performance and usage analytics

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, including to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect and defend our rights or property

  • Protect the personal safety of users or the public

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your information
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and updates
  • Authentication: Phone number verification and secure authentication systems
Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications or location services

7.2 Location Services

You can control location permissions through your device settings:

  • Disable location services entirely
  • Choose "While Using App" or "Always" permissions
  • Revoke location access at any time

Note: Disabling location services will limit your ability to use core features like emergency alerts and donor matching.

7.3 Communication Preferences

You can manage your notification preferences in the app settings:

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion:
