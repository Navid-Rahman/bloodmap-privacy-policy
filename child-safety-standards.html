Child Safety Standards

BloodMap - Protecting Users, Ensuring Safety
Last Updated: January 30, 2026
Important: BloodMap is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for any behavior that puts children at risk. For urgent safety concerns, contact local authorities immediately.

1. Age Restrictions

BloodMap is designed for adults only (18+). We do not knowingly permit children under 18 to use our service.

2. Content Moderation

All user-generated content is subject to moderation. We prohibit:

  • Inappropriate content targeting minors
  • Sharing of personal information of minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation
  • Harmful or dangerous content

3. Privacy Protections

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18
  • Location data is protected with privacy-first design
  • Users can report inappropriate behavior
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws

4. Communication Safety

  • In-app chat is monitored for safety
  • Users can block and report other users
  • Contact information is not shared without consent
  • Emergency contact information is encouraged

5. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report safety concerns via:

  • In-app report button
  • Email: bloodmapbd@gmail.com
  • We respond within 24-48 hours

6. Staff Training

Our team is trained to:

  • Identify child safety risks
  • Respond to safety reports promptly
  • Escalate serious concerns to authorities

7. Compliance

We comply with:

  • Local child protection laws in Uganda
  • Google Play's child safety policies
  • International child safety standards

8. Contact Information

Email: bloodmapbd@gmail.com

Response Time: 24-48 hours
